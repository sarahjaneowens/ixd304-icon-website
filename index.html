About the gang

I am glad to present to you the funny and colourful creation of The Masked Monsters.

These little guys are monsters who love to play games and have a lot of fun, as you can see. However, their favourite hobby is cosplay.This is because these monsters like to go incognito and disguise themselves as well known fictional film, book or video game characters.
